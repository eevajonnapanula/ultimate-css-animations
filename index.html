Ultimate frisbee plays

This is a little project for learning some CSS-animations. As I play ultimate frisbee, and we often use drawings to demonstrate tactics, I wanted to try out if I could produce some plays with pure CSS. Obviously, I couldn't use our teams plays, so I had to use Google to find something. Link to original plays from Playspedia can be found after every animation.

If you are prefer reduced motion (and have enabled these settings from your operating system): These animations have been disabled, and right now there's no other explanation for what's happening in the animation. I'm sorry.
